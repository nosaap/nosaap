Welcome

NOSAAP Producer Company is one of the producer company comprising of small and marginal farmers.  The farmers are linked to input companies, markets and technical and professional institutions to enable all round development of the farmers.Namsai Organic Spices and Agricultural Products Producer Company is one of the producer companies comprising of small and marginal farmers. The farmers are linked to input companies, markets and technical and professional institutions to enable all round development of the farmers. NOSAAP Producer Company Limited was registered on April, 2018. The producer company currently has 29 FIG’s (Farmer Interest Groups) comprising of 521 farmers across Namsai, Arunachal Pradesh. It has 11 Board of Directors representing various blocks and Farmer Interest Groups (FIG’s). Since its inception, the Farmer Producer Company (FPC) has been highly active and shares a high level of bondage and understanding amongst the farming community. The identified farmer’s take a high level of pride and ownership towards the FPC and involve actively in the meetings and program activities.

The farmers are doing application of these organic manures.  Value added products such as 6 variety Pickles, Banana Chips, Ginger candy release in the market. It is performing quite well especially Banana Chips.  Procurement of Black Rice to the farmers for cultivation as it is highly nutritious rice and highly demanded rice product in the market  Training of Bamboo made Polyhouse and Bee Keeping provided to the farmers in association with KVK, Tengapani Our Outreach No. of Villages ⇒ 29 No. of District ⇒ 1 (Namsai, AP) No. of Farmer Interest Group(FIG) ⇒ 29 No. of Farmer Members ⇒ 521 No. of Shareholders ⇒ 400 Our Unique Selling Proposition (USP)  Our farmer’s land is located in the foothills of the eastern Himalayas having high fertile land where all the crops are grown organically with minimal or no use of any type of chemical pesticide  Any suitable crops grown here flourished very nicely making the area most preferred zone of investment in the field of agriculture as it is devoid of any flood or drought with high fertility  Our farmers own approximately 2000 hectares of land who believe in group cultivation.
