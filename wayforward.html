Way Forward

  • Machinery Hub – Project submitted to Govt. for setting up of machinery hub in the village to minimize the cultivation cost of the FPO farmers.
  • Chilli & Turmeric food processing unit – Plan to set up processing unit under the brand of the FPO with the support of Govt of AP.
  • Cultivation of high value crop– To give better earning to the farmers high value crops are selected for production.
  • Promotion of Rajendra Sonia Turmeric – Two Model nursery for Rajendra Sonia Turmeric having production of 42 MT per hectares have been established so that other farmers can do the cultivation at minimal cost from next financial year
